Our Mission

Our Mission is simple: provide local produce to the community to improve health, bolster our local economy, and reduce food miles.

What We Do

Support your local economy, reduce carbon footprint, save the planet, and save money by eating local..

What We Offer

From Earth To Table offers the following services:

      fresh boxed organic produce from local farmers
      grass fed beef
      raw honey from local bees
      Amish butter
      unpasteurized milk

Why We Do This

From Earth To Table supports the localvore movement. We believe that eating locally sustains us all. This is an international movement as evidenced by this blog post on seven benefits of eating local from a blogger in the United Kingdom.
